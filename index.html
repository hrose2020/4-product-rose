Andover Bookstore

About

The Andover Bookstore, located in Andover, Massachusetts, was founded in 1809. It is the second oldest bookstore in the country, and celebrated its 200th anniversary in October 2009. The store was bought by the late Robert Hugo in 1992. It is one of multiple locations of HugoBooks, including Marblehead, Newburyport, Beverly, and a clothing store, Campus Collections, all owned by the Hugo family. The Andover Bookstore is the perfect place to go for storytelling, author events, book groups, and poetry readings. The store promotes shopping local and supporting small businesses instead of large corporations. The Andover location is one of the only bookstores of its kind around the area, which makes it so special to the town. In ___ the Andover Bookstore moved to a new and cozier location also on Main Street. Townspeople still come in and reminisce about their childhood memories in the store. Others come in and make new memories.
